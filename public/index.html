Scatterize reference

Statistical models

OLS
Standard linear regression. Fits a line minimizing the sum of squared errors. Sensitive to outliers.
Robust
MM-estimation with Tukey biweight weighting. Automatically downweights outliers and resists high-leverage points that would distort an ordinary fit.
Spearman
Rank-based correlation. Measures monotonic association without assuming linearity or normality.
Theil-Sen
Median-based slope estimator. Highly robust to outliers; uses the median of all pairwise slopes.

Smoothers

Running median
Local medians in a sliding window. Robust to outliers; reveals trends without assuming a shape.
LOWESS
Locally weighted regression. A smooth curve fitted independently at each point.

Censoring

Click any point to censor it — exclude it from the regression while keeping it visible as a grey hollow marker. Click again to restore it. Use censoring to explore how individual observations affect your results.

Privacy & sharing

Everything runs in your browser — your data is never sent to a server. If you load a public CSV or Google Sheet URL, all settings (variables, model, censored points) are encoded in the URL. Share that URL and anyone with access to the data will see the same view.
